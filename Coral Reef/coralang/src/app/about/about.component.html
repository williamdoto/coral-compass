About Us

Our Mission

With the increasing threat of global warming, it's devastating impacts are felt on the frontlines by our planet's diverse range of flora and fauna. And our project is created to help raise awareness regarding the gravity of the issue. Using this platform, you can see an interactive map where you can find the various stress points of coral reefs all across the globe. We hope you can use this platform to good use, and help spread the impact of global warming not only to coral reefs, but to various societies around the world.
